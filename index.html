ESG

Investment for Better World

ESG investing involves investments on stocks of firms with high rankings on their sustainable practices which make positive environmental, social, and governance impacts. The score rubrics for these rankings normally are based on the UN Sustainable Development Goals which acknowledge that significant private sector action is needed to foster the goals. The US SIF provides some examples of corporate activities causing ESG impacts.
